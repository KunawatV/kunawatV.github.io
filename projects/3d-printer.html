3D Printed Speaker // University Project

Student

2019

Project Brief

Create a 3D printer from scratch, and design and make a speaker with the 3D printer.

This involves the usage of open source materials and designs. Alongside the making of the 3D printer, the understanding of different 3D printer fillaments and settings were needed. This was furthermore pushed in the making of the speaker where the design is made to be 3D printed.
