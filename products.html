Our Products

We aim to create thoughtful education technology built on intuitive interaction with math, supporting effective learning outcomes.

Do Math Good

Do Math Good helps teachers develop student intuition with algebra and grow their confidence with maths.

It's the perfect fun and interactive inclass resource for Math Teachers to spark discussion and deliver personalised learning for arithmetic, algebra and algebra skills.

Learn more

Manic Math

Manic Math aims to make Math fun with intuitive interactions and engaging everyone in the class.

It's the game to play in class to help getting every student engaged and have fun while doing math.

This project is under development and we would love to hear your ideas!.
