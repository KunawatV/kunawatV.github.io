About Us

We believe that technology can make a big impact in supporting students. Therefore we want to share our knowledge to help teachers bring them into the classroom. We aim to help teachers stay updated on the latest technology available and how to use it in class. Furthermore we want to connect teachers with developers to help co-create the future of education technology.

Our Mission

To creates and advocates thoughtful education technology built on fluid interactions with math ,supporting effective learning outcomes.

Our Vision

We believe that technology has an untapped potential to improve student engagement and outcomes in the classroom.

We work with teachers to help students build confidence in their Maths ability through creating thoughtful Math Tech. We are always open for discussions and feedbacks. Feel free to drop us a message, like our Math Mate Facebook page or email us at team@mate.education.
