Professional Development

We believe that technology can make a big impact in supporting students. Therefore we want to share our knowledge to help teachers bring them into the classroom. We aim to help teachers stay updated on the latest technology available and how to use it in class. Furthermore we want to connect teachers with developers to help co-create the future of education technology.

EdTech in Class

In this course we will introduce various available education technology, but more importantly about how to use these technology to support and advance your students. Using these as the basis, we hope that each teacher understands and apply the different ways of cooperating technology into their classroom.

Math Mate Products

As an Education Technology firm we also create our own Math Tech products. In this course we will introduce and show teacher different ways of using our product in class to help promote engagement in class and support students in and outside of class.
